Steal The Grinch

This game is where you steal "Grinch tokens" from other players. No need to buy anything. You only need to participate in the game.

    Here are the rules:

  1. If you get a Grinch token, you must keep it on you at all times.
  2. Steal a Grinch token. Through out the evening you simply ask someone for a Grinch.
    • “Hey, quick thing — do you have a Grinch?”
    • “Before I forget, are you holding a Grinch right now?”
    • “Can I see your Grinch for a second?”
  3. If you are asked for a Grinch and you have one you MUST give the Grinch to that person.
  4. You are not allowed to ask for Grinch back from that person that took it from you.
  5. If you witness the crime (someone getting a Grinch from someone else), you must wait at least 10 minutes to ask them for that Grinch.
  6. You cannot hold more than one Grinch at a time. When the game is over and if you have more than one Grinch you are disqualified.
  7. This game will be over at the start of the White Elephant game. I will be giving some gifts for those Grinch tokens.
  8. If you do not want to participate, simply do not ask for a Grinch and just tell people; "Bah humbug!"
